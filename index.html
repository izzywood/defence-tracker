How UK Defence Enterprise Works

Government Structure

Ministry of Defence (MoD)

Overall responsibility for UK defence policy and armed forces

Defence Equipment & Support (DE&S)

Procurement and support of military equipment

Defence Science & Technology Laboratory (Dstl)

Research and development for defence

Procurement Process

1

Requirements Definition

MoD defines operational requirements and capability gaps

2

Competition & Tender

DE&S runs competitive procurement processes

3

Contract Award

Prime contractors selected based on capability and value

4

Delivery & Support

Ongoing delivery, testing, and through-life support
